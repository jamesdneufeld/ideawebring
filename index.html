👋✨ Welcome to the IDEA Web Ring!

A web ring is like a TikTok collab chain, but for websites. 🕸️✨ It’s a group of sites linked together around a shared vibe or topic (like art, memes, or tech). You click through them in a loop, discovering new spots on the internet—kinda like doomscrolling, but cooler and more intentional. Old-school internet energy, but still lowkey fire. 🔥
