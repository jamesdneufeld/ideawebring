IDEA Web Ring Archives

Like many indie-web projects, the IDEA Web Ring evolves over time. Some sites evolve into fully connected web ring projects, while others remain snapshots of experimentation, learning, and early front-end practice. This archive preserves the history of the project and the students who helped shape its early development.
