FROM NOTHING

Growing up, as a kid I was taught it was evil.

People explored it while I worked at an easel.

I finally found it and felt overwhelmed -

I was left behind to drown in the web.

3 Minutes of My Story (>_<)

TO CONSUMTION

As time carried on I adjusted my view.

The maze became somewhere I slowly walked through.

But my art lived on paper and made me feel small;

The digital world still looked like a wall.

TO CREATION

I broke down the lessons, one step at a time.

Not knowing the basics felt like a crime.

But like every form my creations can be,

The journey is long, but success makes me free.
