✎ ludwig van beethoven

after learning piano for around a decade, i decided to try learning cello. since i wasn't very familiar with cello repertoire at the time, i did some exploring and this is the first cello sonta i fell in love with. my favourite aspects are that the piano and cello are equally interesting in their own right, but also complement each other so well it's almost conversational. also, i like how beethoven makes use of the cello's expansive range. unfortunately, i'm currently unable to play this piece, but i hope to in the future!
